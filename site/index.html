AI can ship faster than teams can verify.

AI coding agents create PRs, fixes, and features quickly. But merge confidence still needs human evidence: what was checked, who checked it, what proof exists, and why the work is safe to move forward.

?

Review uncertainty

Teams struggle to know whether an AI-generated change was actually checked in a real browser, device, or workflow.

↯

Scattered proof

Screenshots, QA notes, review comments, and release decisions get buried across chats, PRs, docs, and issue trackers.

✓

No clear trace

When something breaks, teams need to know what was verified, what evidence existed, and who accepted the proof.

One clean proof loop.

ProofTask turns AI-generated work into a structured verification flow that ends with a traceable decision and a PR-ready recommendation.

What the proof includes.

The output is not another vague “LGTM”. It is a structured artifact that supports merge, release, recheck, or rejection decisions.

  • What was checked
  • Who checked it
  • What evidence exists
  • Who accepted or rejected it
  • Why it is safe to move forward
