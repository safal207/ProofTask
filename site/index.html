01 / The value

Speed is easy.
Accountability is rare.

AI can generate changes in minutes. ProofTask gives the human decision the same level of structure: explicit criteria, attached evidence, named verification, and a trace that survives the chat window.

Turn verification into a durable product artifact.

Replace scattered screenshots and vague comments with one coherent record connecting the task, environment, human proof, decision, and recommendation.

Make confidence explainable.

When a change is challenged later, the team can see what was tested, by whom, in which environment, and why the decision was accepted.

02 / The workflow

One clean proof loop.
No ceremony.

A narrow sequence that creates accountability without turning verification into another heavyweight process.

01

AI creates the change

A coding agent opens a PR or modifies a user-facing flow.

02

Criteria become explicit

The verification task states exactly what must be checked and what proof is required.

03

A human submits evidence

Result, environment, notes, and evidence references are attached to the task.

04

A verifier decides

The proof is accepted, rejected, or returned for a recheck with a recorded reason.

05

The team gets a recommendation

A concise PR-ready output explains the status, evidence, and next decision.

03 / The artifact

A decision people can inspect.

The value is not another green badge. It is a readable record of what was checked and why the team can move forward.
